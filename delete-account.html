Delete your account

Two ways to do it — in the app, or by email.

On this page

Delete in the app Request by email What gets deleted What is kept

Delete in the app (fastest)

  1. Open Trovro and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile → Settings.
  3. Tap Request account deletion at the bottom.
  4. Pick a reason and confirm. Your account is scheduled for deletion and removed after 6 months of inactivity — signing in again cancels it automatically.

Request by email

If you can't access the app, email trovro01@gmail.com from the address on your account, with the subject "Delete my Trovro account".

We verify the request comes from the account holder, then delete within 7 days and confirm by email.

What gets deleted

  • Your name, email address and mobile number
  • Your profile photo, bio, headline, prices and service areas
  • Your saved address details
  • Your payout details (UPI ID or QR image)
  • Your sign-in credentials — you can no longer log in
  • Any identity document you uploaded (these are deleted as soon as verification is decided, in any case)

What is kept, and why

Bookings and reviews that form part of another user's history are retained, dissociated from you where possible. A professional's job history and a customer's booking record would otherwise be destroyed by the other party leaving.

We never sell your data, and we hold no payment information — you always pay professionals directly.
